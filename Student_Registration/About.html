About us


Merganser University is an online learning aid provided to instructors and students. It is completely open source and allows a clean interface where an instructor may provide course material to students in an organized fashion. Learning is not easy and having a clear and concise portrayal of content can make all the difference.

Online Assistance



How many times have you felt the fear of failing a class? Merganser University provides students and instructors with an interface that allows them to interact through our messaging app. Also! It is not very often where you find yourself alone in any class, use our Q and A forum to interact with other students and see what they think.


Evaluated Projects



Immediate feedback is crucial to the development of your skills as an up and coming code ninja. Your projects are evaluated and graded based off how your instructor thinks you did. Constructive criticism is important as well as learning of our shortcomings. Your instructor can post graded assignments with feed back easily for your growth.


Test Preporation



Do you not feel ready for your next test? Fear Not! Merganser University is here to save you from your test anxiety. Your instructor can highlight key points to lectures and provide footnotes to those lectures. An Interactive study guide is also provided where the student will be able to take practice tests to see where there is room for improvement.
